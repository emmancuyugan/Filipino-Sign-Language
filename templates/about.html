Meet Our Team

Passionate innovators building the future of Filipino Sign Language education through AI-powered technology

Our Mission

Building Inclusive Technology

We're creating an innovative AI-powered platform that makes Filipino Sign Language learning accessible, interactive, and effective. Our system combines advanced LSTM neural networks with intuitive design to create a comprehensive learning experience.

Key Features

  • Provide real-time recognition and visual feedback for FSL gestures
  • Offer instructional videos to guide users in learning FSL
  • Deliver corrective feedback to help users improve their performance
